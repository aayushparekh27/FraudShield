Prevention Tips for Cyber Fraud

Follow these expert-recommended tips to stay safe from online scams and protect your personal information from fraudsters.

🛡️ Use Strong Passwords

Passwords are the first line of defense. Use long, complex passwords and avoid reusing the same password across multiple accounts. Consider using a password manager to store and generate secure passwords.

🔑 Enable Two-Factor Authentication

Two-factor authentication (2FA) provides an additional layer of security. Even if someone obtains your password, they won't be able to log in without the second factor (usually a code sent to your phone).

📧 Be Cautious with Emails & Links

Phishing emails are one of the most common ways fraudsters steal your data. Never click on suspicious links, and always verify the authenticity of the email sender before engaging.

🔍 Regularly Monitor Your Accounts

Check your bank statements, credit card transactions, and online accounts frequently. Early detection of suspicious activity can help you stop fraud before it escalates.
